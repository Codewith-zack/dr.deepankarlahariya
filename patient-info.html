Patient Information

Everything you need to know for your visit

When to Visit

If you experience any of the following symptoms, please consult the doctor:

  • Persistent fever or high temperature
  • Severe headache or dizziness
  • Chest pain or breathing difficulties
  • Uncontrolled diabetes or blood pressure
  • Severe injuries or wounds
  • Chronic health conditions requiring monitoring

What to Bring

  • Previous medical records or prescriptions
  • Any current medications
  • Insurance information (if applicable)
  • ID proof for registration

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are preferred, walk-ins are also welcome based on availability.

What are the consultation fees?

Fees vary based on the type of consultation. Please contact the clinic for details.

Is the clinic equipped for emergencies?

The clinic handles general medical emergencies. For critical cases, we recommend visiting the nearest hospital.
